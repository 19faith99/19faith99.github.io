College stress is something that everyone can find a tie to. Whether you're in college, you have a freind or family member in college, or you have regualr interactions with a college student, stress from college is in your life one way or another.

The American Instute of Stress reports that the two most common reasons college students seek mental helath serivices are anxiety and depression, and most times the root cause both are stress.

This semster, I have delved into the world of college stress; what it means, what it stems from, and who it effects. Below is two blog posts, a podcast, and a video that share what I have learned.
